CRM for Timperio

We were tasked by Timperio, an olive oil company, to create an efficient CRM system for them that contains insightful visualisations to inform staff about customers and their purchasing patterns. In addition, we needed to provide customisable newsletters that the marketing team can use to reach out to customers. Lastly, as a CRM system, we had to ensure that admins can easily create and manage staff accounts.

Throughout the project, we utilised various design principles, such as the 'Single Responsibility Principle' and 'Separation of Concerns'. In particular, we focused on core OOP principles such as abstraction, encapsulation, inheritence as well as polymorphism.

This project uses next.js, react and tailwind for its frontend, and java for its backend. Various open source libraries like bcrypt, spring security, lombok, radix ui and lucid. ðŸ«’

Sales staff can view the monthly, quarterly and yearly sales breakdown as well as view a summary of the sales statistics on their home page.

They are also able to filter transactions by variables like sales type, platform and customer id to easily retrieve the details of a specific transaction.

The sales and marketing teams can also view a list of all customers who have previously shopped at timperio. This shows customer details like ID, zip code and tier. They can also filter the customers by tier or sort by spending.

They can click on a particular customer to view further information about the the customer's most purchased products and purchase history.

Marketing staff can also customise newsletter templates and send them out as emails, either to specific customers or as mass emails.

Last but not least, admin staff are able to perform CRUD operations for staff accounts using this page.
