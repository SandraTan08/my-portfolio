Kampuni

Kampuni is a project that aims to solve common issues faced in coliving situations. Whether it be delegation of household chores, expense splitting or finding bonding time, there are many issues that coinhabitants may face.

Hence, we came up with a solution to make coliving a bit easier for everyone! Our web app helps with optimising chore allocation (ensuring that there are no slackers in the house ðŸ¤«), outing coordination and expense management.

This app uses javascript, css, tailwind, vue.js and firebase.

Here is the chat function of the web app. There are groupchats that allow the coinhabitants to coordinate and plan their outings.

Users will also be able to see their upcoming outings and tasks on their calendar.

They can create expenses to track bills and determine who has yet to settle the costs.
